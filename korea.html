Korea

I Love Korea.

Korea has a small place in my heart. It provided me a distraction when I was going through a hard time in my life and I am grateful for that.

--

My interest started out with an accidental exposure to Kpop and intensified after finding out about Korean shows.

--

Ever since, I always want to learn more about Korean culture and the language and am enjoying every bit of it.

K-Entertainment

K-pop

A big reason why I love Kpop is because of the music and choreography.

Yes, pop is the same no matter what the language it's in. But why Korean pop, you might ask. Answer: Just cause :). And no, I don't understand most of the lyrics, but that's what makes it better.

From my observation, most American pop songs don't come with a choreo. Dance is one of my hobbies and I love dancing to songs that I like; it's better when there is a dance associated with the song.

K-dramas/K-shows

It is nice watching good-looking people on the screen and living vicariously through the actors, but that's not the only reason I watch Korean dramas and shows.

Through Korean entertainment, I am able to learn more about Korean culture and language.

Studying Abroad

I had the privilege to studying abroad at Yonsei University during the winter of 2016. During the three weeks, I studied Korean 1 and psychology for half of the time and explored Seoul for the other.

Things I've Gained from this Experience

  • Knowledge of cultures other than Korean
  • Fluency in Korean
  • More willingness to try new things
  • A level in my adapting skills
  • Weight from all the delicious street food consumed
