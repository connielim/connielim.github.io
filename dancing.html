Honing a Skill

Even though I dance as a hobby, it has also honed my observation and perfection skill. Dancing has taught me to perfect the position of every body part of each move such as the angle, how far out the arm should be, how much energy should be put into it, and so much more. A successful dance cover to me is when you place your dance cover and the original dance side by side and notice little to no difference between the two.
